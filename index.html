Sonic Surfer(2020)

Sonic Surfer is a multimedia game inspired early iphone-games such as temple run and flappy bird. The game is controled thorugh a pressure senstive pad that the player stands on. Sonic Surfer relies primarily on spatial audio information encoded for a pair of headphones. The user leans towards "good" sounds and avoids the bad "ones".

Feedback(2020)

Feedback is a multimodal instrument inspired by the electro-acoustic phenomenon, feedback. This instrument uses gloves with embedded light sensors to control audio synthesis and subsequently control a 2D animation. The user then uses this animation to excite the gloves sensors.

Great Green Room(2019)

The self-produced debut album from my band, Reference Section. This record was recorded by myself and Fisher Diede and mixed by Fisher Diede. Great Green Room was recorded at the Duderstadt Center Audio Studio.
